
> [!NOTE] Formula
> $$ \text{biased exponent } = \text{ actual exponent } + \text{bias} $$
> $$ \text{bias for single precision (32 bit) is 127} $$


> [!NOTE] Why IEEE standard uses a biased exponent instead of two's complement
>  Non-negative floating-point numbers can be compared directly as if they were integers for comparison purposes
